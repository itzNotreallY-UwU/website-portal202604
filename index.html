📖 Step-by-Step User Guide (First Time)
  1. Explore Carousels – Use left/right arrows on the main carousel.
  2. Infinite Scroll Social Media – Watch the auto-scrolling social media bar. Hover to pause and click any icon to follow us!
  3. Student Resources – Click "Preview & Launch" to access learning platforms.
  4. Feedback Form – Enter Full Name, Matrix Number (only digits), and valid Email (must contain @ and .). Green tick appears when correct.
  5. Submit with Confirmation – Click Submit → Yes/No modal → Success modal appears.
  6. Navbar & Scroll – Navbar hides when scrolling down, appears on scroll up. Purple hover on links.

💡 Pro tip: Matrix numbers accept digits only, email must include '@' and '.' for validation.
